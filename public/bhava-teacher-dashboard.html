Class-Level Teaching Tips

By Subject
🔢 Mathematics IQ Focus

Start each Maths lesson with a 2-minute warm-up puzzle — patterns, sequences, or number games. This wakes up logical thinking for the whole class without adding to your preparation time.

📖 English / Language EQ Focus

Use "story feelings" discussions: ask students how a character felt and why. This naturally develops empathy and expressive communication. One question per lesson is enough — no extra planning needed.

🔬 Science SQ + IQ

Group experiments work best when roles are assigned — observer, recorder, presenter. Rotating roles across sessions builds leadership, cooperation, and logical thinking simultaneously.

🎨 Art & Physical Education EQ + SQ

Creative and physical activities are natural EQ and SQ builders. Let students design their own rules for a game or activity — it builds social awareness, conflict resolution, and confidence organically.
